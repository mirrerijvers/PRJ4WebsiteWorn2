Where we source 

In the Atacama desert in Chili, lies the biggest lies the largest clothing dump in the world. Every year 39,000 tons of broken clothes arrive. As of the end of 2021, there are already more than 100,000 tons of abandoned clothing. Still, the world continues to produce and make new clothes. This process continues endlessly, and this cycle must be broken. Within Worn, we make sure every piece of clothing our customers purchase has been worn, and thus nothing goes to waste.
